IV001 Approach

A new approach to experience design.

Interval is a global strategic advisory and design lab developing transformational fan, audience, visitor and customer experiences through adaptable spaces and places — via a Total Experience Design™ approach.

Spaces and places need to be more multi-modal than ever before to deliver transformational experiences for everyone, and greater returns on investment. The creative thinking, technology, content and ambition is available — but the process and full-spectrum framework is not.

We exist to close that gap. Quietly, rigorously, end-to-end.

Today, ambition is rarely the limiting factor. Capital is available. Technology is mature. Audiences expect more — and are willing to reward those who deliver.

What's missing is a connective tissue: a single framework that holds spatial, technological, brand, content and human dimensions together across the full lifecycle of a place. Interval was founded to provide that connective tissue.

IV002 — The Framework

Total Experience Design™ holds the whole picture together.

Five pillars. Four phases. One continuous process.

Total Experience Design™ is a complete operating system for places. It binds the five disciplines that shape how a space feels — Spatial, Technology, Brand & Digital, Content, and Time — to the four phases that govern how it gets made: Vision, Master Planning, Realisation, and the Human Foundation it all returns to.

Time sits at the centre because experiences unfold. A place is not a product shipped on a date; it is a system that must perform from opening day through every season that follows.

IV004 — The Process

Four phases, anchored to the human.

PH / 01
Human Foundation
Every project begins and ends with the people it is for. We define the audiences, the operational teams, and the stakeholders whose lives the place will touch — and we build the brief from their reality, not from precedent.
PH / 02
Vision
A sharpened, defendable point of view on what the space must become — and why it matters now. This is where ambition gets articulated in a form that survives contact with reality.
PH / 03
Master Planning
Translating vision into a buildable, fundable, operable plan across all five pillars simultaneously — so spatial, technology, brand, content and time decisions reinforce rather than contradict one another.
PH / 04
Realisation
Holding the line from groundbreaking through opening and into ongoing operation. We stay close enough to protect intent, and step back early enough that the client's team can run it.
